Minecraft: Shader Pack Ultra

Version 1.3.2 Updated: 2023-07-22 Minecraft 1.20+

Description

Transform your Minecraft world with this ultra-realistic shader pack featuring dynamic lighting, volumetric clouds, realistic water reflections, and stunning atmospheric effects. This shader pack pushes Minecraft's graphics to the limit while maintaining playable frame rates on mid-range systems.

The pack includes 10 preset configurations ranging from "Lite" (for low-end PCs) to "Extreme" (for high-end gaming rigs). All settings can be customized in-game through the shader options menu.

Features

  • Dynamic per-pixel lighting with realistic shadows
  • Volumetric clouds that react to weather and time
  • Reflective water with waves and caustics
  • Physically-based materials (PBR) for blocks
  • Atmospheric scattering for realistic skies
  • Dynamic foliage movement
  • Adjustable depth of field
  • 10 performance presets
  • Works with OptiFine and Iris

Requirements

  • Minecraft Version: 1.16.5 - 1.20.1
  • Mod Loader: OptiFine HD U H7 or Iris 1.6.0+
  • OS: Windows 10/11, macOS, Linux
  • CPU: Intel Core i5 or equivalent
  • RAM: 4GB allocated to Minecraft
  • GPU: NVIDIA GTX 1050 / AMD RX 560 or better
  • Storage: 500MB additional space

Installation Instructions

Install OptiFine or Iris

Download and install either OptiFine HD U H7 or Iris 1.6.0+ for your Minecraft version.

Download the Shader Pack

Click the download button above to get the shader pack ZIP file.

Install the Shaders

Launch Minecraft, go to Options > Video Settings > Shaders, and click "Shaders Folder". Place the downloaded ZIP file in this folder.

Activate the Shaders

Back in Minecraft, select "Shader Pack Ultra" from the shaders list and click "Done".

Adjust Settings

Use the in-game shader options to select a preset or customize individual settings for optimal performance and visuals.
