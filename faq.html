Frequently Asked Questions

GameModHub is a cyberpunk-themed platform where gamers can upload, download, and request mods for their favorite games. We provide a community-driven space for mod enthusiasts to share their creations.

Installation methods vary by game and mod type. Generally:

  1. Download the mod file
  2. Extract the contents if it's a ZIP file
  3. Follow the instructions provided by the mod creator
  4. Most mods require placing files in a specific game directory

Always check the mod description for specific installation instructions.

We strive to maintain a safe environment by scanning uploads for malware. However:

  • Only download mods from trusted creators
  • Read user comments and ratings
  • Use antivirus software to scan downloads
  • Backup your game files before installing mods

GameModHub is not responsible for any issues caused by mods.

To upload a mod:

  1. Go to the Upload Mod page
  2. Fill in all required information about your mod
  3. Upload your mod files (ZIP format recommended)
  4. Optionally add screenshots or a thumbnail
  5. Submit your mod for review

Once approved, your mod will be available to the community.

  • Game name
  • Type of mod (graphics, gameplay, items, etc.)
  • Specific features you'd like
  • Any reference images or examples

Our community of modders may choose to fulfill your request.

Mod creators put in a lot of work, and there are several ways to support them:

  • Leave positive comments and ratings on their mods
  • Share their work on social media
  • Check if they have donation links (Patreon, Ko-fi, etc.)
  • Report bugs and provide constructive feedback
  • Credit them if you showcase their mod in videos or streams
