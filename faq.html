Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install mods?

+

Most mods come with installation instructions. Generally, you'll need to extract the mod files to your game's installation directory or use a mod manager. Always check the mod's description for specific instructions.

Is modding safe?

+

Modding is generally safe, but always download mods from trusted sources. We scan all uploads for malware, but it's still good practice to have antivirus software. Always back up your game saves before installing mods.

Can I upload mods I didn't create?

+

Only if you have explicit permission from the original creator. We respect intellectual property rights and will remove unauthorized uploads.

Why was my mod rejected?

+

Mods may be rejected for various reasons including: containing malicious code, violating copyright, being incomplete, or not following our content guidelines. You'll receive a notification with the reason if your mod is rejected.

How do I report a problematic mod?

+

Each mod page has a "Report" button. Click it and fill out the form to let us know about any issues with the mod. Our team will review it promptly.
